User Guide

Introduction

Welcome to our application! This User Guide provides you with all the information you need to get the most out of our app. It includes details on how to use key features, as well as answers to frequently asked questions.

Key Features
  • Manage Users: Admins can add, edit, activate, deactivate, and delete users.
  • Manage Links: Admins can add, edit, delete and manage ownership of links.
Managing Users

To manage users, navigate to the 'Manage Users' page. Here you can see a table of all users, their email addresses, roles, and whether they are currently active.

To add a new user: Click the 'Add User' button, fill in the details in the form, and click 'Save User'.

To edit a user: Click the 'Edit' button in the user's row, make changes in the form, and click 'Save Changes'.

To deactivate a user: Click the 'Deactivate User' button in the user's row.

To activate a user: Click the 'Activate User' button in the user's row.

To delete a user: Click the 'Delete' button in the user's row, then confirm your decision in the pop-up window.

Managing Links

Our application allows you to manage multiple links. Here's how you can make the most of this feature:

To add a new link: Navigate to the 'Manage Links' page and click on the 'Add Link' button. Provide the necessary details in the form and click 'Save Link'.

To edit a link: On the 'Manage Links' page, find the link you want to edit and click the 'Edit' button in the corresponding row. Make your changes in the form that appears and then click 'Save Changes'.

To delete a link: On the 'Manage Links' page, click the 'Delete' button in the row of the link you wish to remove. Confirm your decision in the pop-up window.

FAQs

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
